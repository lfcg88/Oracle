

++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
The DBA_TEMP_FREE_SPACE view shows the total free space available, including the space currently allocated to a temporary tablespace and available for reuse as well as space that’s currently unallocated.
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++


select * from dba_temp_free_space;

++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
 command to shrink a temporary tablespace
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

SQL> alter tablespace temp shrink space;


++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
To shrink a tempfile
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

SQL> alter tablespace temp shrink
tempfile '/u01/app/oracle/oradata/or11/temp01.dbf';
